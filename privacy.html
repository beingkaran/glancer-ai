Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ObservaPedia ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you visit our website (the "Site"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices. By using the Site, you consent to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

If you contact us through our contact form or by email, we receive the information you choose to send — typically your name, email address and message. We use this solely to respond to you and to improve the glossary.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our hosting provider and analytics tools may automatically collect standard technical information such as your browser type, device type, approximate location (derived from IP address), referring pages and the pages you visit. This helps us understand how the Site is used and keep it running reliably.

Information stored on your device

We store a small preference in your browser's local storage to remember your light/dark theme choice. This never leaves your device and is not used to identify you.

2. Cookies and similar technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device. We and our partners may use cookies and similar technologies to operate the Site, remember preferences, measure traffic, and — where advertising is enabled — to serve and personalize ads. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings; doing so may affect some features.

We may display advertising on the Site, including through Google AdSense. To support this, please note the following:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our Site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising at www.aboutads.info/choices and youronlinechoices.eu.
  • Third-party ad networks may use the Google "DART" cookie and similar technologies to serve ads based on a user's visit to our Site and other sites on the Internet.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our Site, see Google's "How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services".

4. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics services to understand aggregate usage trends. These tools report statistics in aggregate and are not used to personally identify individual visitors.

5. How we use information

  • To operate, maintain and improve the Site and its content.
  • To respond to your enquiries and feedback.
  • To measure and analyze traffic and usage trends.

  • To detect, prevent and address technical or security issues.

6. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who help us operate the Site (such as hosting, analytics and advertising partners), and where required by law. These providers are expected to handle data consistently with this policy and applicable law.

7. Your rights and choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. Residents of the European Economic Area (GDPR) and California (CCPA/CPRA) may have additional rights. To exercise any of these, contact us at hello@observapedia.com. You can also manage cookies and ad personalization through your browser and the ad-settings links above.

The Site is intended for a general, professional audience and is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Data security and retention

We take reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described here or as required by law.

12. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@observapedia.com or via our contact page.
